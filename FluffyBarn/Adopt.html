Are You Ready to Adopt Your New Best Friend from Fluffy Barn?

Our adoption center welcomes you to Fluffy Barn, where we open our doors to help you find your new furry companion. Our adoption hours are Thursdays and Fridays from 4-7 pm, Saturdays and Sundays from 12-6 pm. To streamline the process, please complete an application before your visit. No appointment is needed, and our team looks forward to assisting you during open hours.

Explore the currently available dogs and cats under Adoptable Dogs and Adoptable Cats. We regularly update these pages as new animals become available or find loving homes.

All animals have dedicated applications that you can submit directly through their profiles. If you're undecided, a general application is also available. We are excited to help you find your new companion!

For specific inquiries about our dogs and cats, please email:
- Cats: cats@fluffybarn.org
- Dogs: dogs@fluffybarn.org

Adoption Requirements:

To adopt, you must be 21 years of age or older with a valid government-issued ID. Renters will need a current lease agreement or landlord contact information. Condo owners may need to provide condo association details. We encourage all household members to visit with the animal before adoption.

Steps to Adopt:

  1. Visit the shelter to meet our adoptable pets.
  2. Complete a dog or cat adoption application online. Note that we do not "hold" animals.
  3. We reserve the right to deny adoption.

Adoption Fees:

  • Puppies (8 weeks to 6 months): $400
  • Dogs (6 months to 8 years, small/med-large): $300/$250
  • Dogs (8 years and up): $150 or free for adopters aged 65+
  • Kittens (up to 6 months): $125
  • Pair of kittens: $200
  • Cats (6 months to 8 years): $100
  • Cats (8 years and up): $75 or free for adopters aged 65+

Adoption fees cover vaccinations, deworming, tests, microchip, spay/neuter, and a "Newly Adopted Health Certificate" for a free first wellness exam, grooming discount, and one night of boarding.

At Fluffy Barn, you're not "buying" a pet; you're supporting the time, care, and medical attention given to each animal. We operate as a no-kill shelter and a non-profit organization, relying on donations to rescue and care for pets.
